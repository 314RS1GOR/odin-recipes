Lasagna

Description: Lasagnas are one of the most appreciated meals of all time

Today, our goal is to make you a real expert in preparing them!


Here is below the list of ingredients you will need:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Now the steps to prepare the dish:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
