Rice

Ingredients:

You will need:

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil
  • And that's all!

Follow these steps:

  1. Wash the rice with cold water
  2. boil water in a saucepan
  3. Put salt in the boiling water
  4. Put a tablespoon of vegetable oil in the water
  5. Put 100g of rice for every liter of water
  6. wait for the rice to absorp all the water
  7. Enjoy!
